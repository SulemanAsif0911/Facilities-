Who We Are

About Us

We are university students initiating a major targeted operation against cyber harassment. Cyber Hayat PK provides a clear guide path for anyone facing cyber harassment: what to save, where to report, who to contact, and how to protect personal accounts while getting help.

Action Path

Guide Path

01

Save the Evidence

Keep screenshots, URLs, usernames, dates, phone numbers, emails, and any messages before blocking the harasser.

02

Secure Your Accounts

Change passwords, enable two factor authentication, review privacy settings, and tell a trusted person what happened.

03

Report and Reach Help

Use official complaint portals, cybercrime helplines, and digital rights support services to move forward safely.

Community Safety

Awareness of Cyber Harassment

Cyber harassment is a serious pattern of intimidation affecting mental health, relationships, and digital security. In Pakistan, safety begins with calm documentation. Save screenshots, links, and user identities immediately before blocking offenders. Secure your digital footprint using multi-factor authentication, and bravely utilize official channels like NCCIA or DRF to protect yourself.
